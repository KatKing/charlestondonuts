About

Charleston Donuts is a unique local, family owned and operated business. Our donuts are always hot andf resh. You will never find a donut case, because our donuts are custom made to order. Design your own masterpiece and watch our friendly staff create it for you. We have so many combinations of toppings you will never run out of options.

We also feature King Bean local Coffee Roasters. We have 13 different varieties of Frappes and Real Fruit Smoothies.

Come in and try our maple and bacon donut or really go crazy with our chocolate and lays potato chips donut. We are different because we use a very unique Donut Robits system. This allows us to cook and serve fresh and hot donuts straight from the fryer
